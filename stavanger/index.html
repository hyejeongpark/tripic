Hike around Stavanger

Kjerag

Kjerag is one of the popular hiking destinations. The Kjeragbolten, a stone wedged between two rocks is located on the mountain.

Preikestolen

Preikestolen, the most famous tourist attraction in Ryfylke, towers an impressive 604 meters over the Lysefjord. It is a great experience to be at the top of the plateau.
